# Insights:

## Overview:
This analysis explores Microsoft stock price data over time, focusing on identifying trends, patterns in price changes, and periods of stability and instability.

---

## Key Findings:
### 1. Strong Upward Trend Over Time:
The data shows a clear upward trend in Microsoft's closing price over the years.

Average yearly prices increase consistently from 2015 to 2025, indicating sustained growth in market value over time.

---

### 2. Long-Term Growth with Short-Term Fluctuations:
While the overall trend is upward, the daily price movements show frequent short-term fluctuations.

These fluctuations are visible in the daily returns, which vary between positive and negative values indicating regular increases and decreases in price.

---

### 3. Moving Averages Reveal Clear Trend Direction:
The 7-day and 30-day moving averages smooth out short-term noise and make the underlying trend easier to observe.

The 7-day moving average reacts more quickly to price changes, while the 30-day moving average shows a more stable long-term trend.

Periods where both moving averages rise together indicate consistent upward movement.

---

### 4. Periods of High and Low Stability:
Volatility analysis shows that the data contains periods of both stability and instability.
- Higher volatility values indicate periods where price changes are more unpredictable.
- Lower volatility values indicate more stable periods with smaller day-to-day changes.

---

### 5. Extreme Values Highlight Significant Market Moments:
The dataset contains clear peaks and dips in price.
- The maximum closing price represents the highest observed value over the dataset period.
- The minimum closing price represents the lowest observed value.

These points help define the overall range and scale of the data.

---

## Summary:
The analysis shows that Microsoft's stock price has experienced strong long-term growth, with regular short-term fluctuations.

Trend analysis through moving averages highlights consistent upward movement, while volatility analysis reveals changing levels of stability over time.

Overall, the data reflects a pattern of sustained growth combined with natural variability in day-to-day price changes.
